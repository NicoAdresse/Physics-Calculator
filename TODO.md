Mechanics (Kinematics & Dynamics)

    Density (DONE)

        rho = mass / volume

        mass = rho * volume

        volume = mass / rho

    Average velocity (DONE)

        velocity = distance / time

        distance = velocity * time

        time = distance / velocity

    Acceleration (DONE)

        acceleration = velocity change / time

        velocity change = acceleration * time

    Force (DONE)

        force = mass * acceleration

        mass = force / acceleration

        acceleration = force / mass

    Weight (DONE)

        weight = mass * gravity

    Work (DONE)

        work = force * distance

    Power (DONE)

        power = work / time

    Momentum (DONE)

        momentum = mass * velocity

    Pressure (DONE)

        pressure = force / area

    Hooke's law (DONE)

        spring force = spring constant * displacement

    Torque (CURRENT)

        torque = force * distance

Energy

    Kinetic energy (DONE)

        kinetic energy = 0.5 * mass * velocity^2

    Potential energy (DONE)

        potential energy = mass * gravity * height

    Heat energy (PENDING)

        heat energy = mass * specific heat capacity * temperature change

    Efficiency (PENDING)

        efficiency = useful output / total input

Electricity & Magnetism

    Ohm's law (PENDING)

        voltage = resistance * current

    Electrical power (PENDING)

    Charge (PENDING)

    Coulomb's law (PENDING)

        electric force = constant * charge1 * charge2 / distance^2
